Our Story

قصتنا

Bayt al-Hikmah was founded with a simple vision: to build Islamic tools and content that are beautiful, trustworthy, and free for every Muslim on earth.

We believe the Ummah deserves world-class digital experiences that help them remember Allah, learn their deen, and build consistent worship habits — without ads hijacking their attention or subscriptions locking them out.

Everything we build is guided by three principles: authenticity (Sahih and Hasan sources only), accessibility (free to every Muslim, regardless of income), and excellence (the highest quality we can produce).

Our Commitment to You

التزامنا تجاهك

No haram content. We do not display ads for gambling, dating, alcohol, music, or anything prohibited in Islam. Every sponsor is manually reviewed.
