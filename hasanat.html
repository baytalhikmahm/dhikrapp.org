🕌 Prayer Rewards
Prayer in Masjid al-Haram
الصلاة في المسجد الحرام
Each prayer in Masjid al-Haram equals 100,000 prayers elsewhere.
100,000×per prayerSunan Ibn Majah 1406 · Sahih ↗
Prayer in Masjid an-Nabawi
الصلاة في المسجد النبوي
Each prayer in the Prophet's Mosque ﷺ equals 1,000 prayers elsewhere.
1,000×per prayerSahih al-Bukhari 1190 · Sahih ↗
Prayer in Masjid al-Aqsa
الصلاة في المسجد الأقصى
Each prayer in al-Aqsa equals 500 prayers elsewhere.
500×per prayerSunan Ibn Majah 1408 · Hasan ↗
Prayer in Congregation
الصلاة في جماعة
Prayer with congregation is 27 degrees (or 25) superior to prayer performed alone.
27×vs praying aloneSahih al-Bukhari 645 · Sahih ↗
2 Sunnah of Fajr
ركعتا الفجر
"The two rak'ahs before Fajr prayer are better than this world and all it contains."
∞better than the worldSahih Muslim 725 · Sahih ↗
📖 Quran Recitation
Every Letter of Quran
كل حرف من القرآن
"Whoever recites a letter from Allah's Book, then he receives the reward from it, and the reward of ten the like of it." Alif is a letter, Lam is a letter, Mim is a letter.
10×per letterJami al-Tirmidhi 2910 · Sahih ↗
Surah Al-Ikhlas
سورة الإخلاص
Reciting Surah Al-Ikhlas once equals one third of the Quran in reward.
⅓ Quranper recitationSahih al-Bukhari 5013 · Sahih ↗
💰 Charity & Sadaqah
Sadaqah — General
الصدقة
"The likeness of those who spend their wealth in the way of Allah is as the likeness of a grain which grows seven ears, in every ear a hundred grains."
700×minimumAl-Quran 2:261 ↗

Ongoing charity that continues after death — building a mosque, planting a tree, teaching knowledge, raising a righteous child.

🌙 Fasting
Every Day of Ramadan Fasting
صيام رمضان
"Whoever fasts Ramadan out of faith and seeking reward, his previous sins will be forgiven."
∞Allah multiplies without limitSahih al-Bukhari 1901 · Sahih ↗
Fasting Monday & Thursday

The Prophet ﷺ fasted on Mondays and Thursdays, saying deeds are presented to Allah on those days.
700×+ deeds reviewed by AllahJami al-Tirmidhi 747 · Sahih ↗
📿 Dhikr
SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu Akbar
سبحان الله · الحمد لله · الله أكبر
Each is rewarded 10 times. Together they fill the scales. "SubhanAllah fills half the Scale, Alhamdulillah fills it completely."
10×per utteranceSahih Muslim 223 · Sahih ↗
La ilaha illallah
لا إله إلا الله
"Whoever says 'La ilaha illallah' sincerely, will enter Paradise." The best dhikr.
∞opens gates of JannahJami al-Tirmidhi 3590 · Sahih ↗

All rewards mentioned are minimums. Allah multiplies for whoever He wills. Sources verified from Sahih and Hasan hadiths only.
