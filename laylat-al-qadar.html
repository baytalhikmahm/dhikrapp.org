The Five Blessed Nights

21st
First Odd Night

The first of the five odd nights. Increase your prayers, Quran recitation, and dhikr from Maghrib until Fajr.

23rd
Second Odd Night

Many scholars and companions would increase their worship especially on this night. Stay awake in prayer and supplication.

25th
Third Odd Night

The middle of the five nights. The Prophet ﷺ would exert himself more in worship during these nights than any other.

27th
Most Likely Night

The 27th night is considered the most likely Laylat al-Qadar by the majority of scholars, based on narrations from the Companions.

29th
Final Odd Night

The last of the five odd nights. Do not let this night pass without maximum worship. Seek forgiveness abundantly.
